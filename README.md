

# 🌱 EcoPlate – AI Powered Food Rescue Platform

EcoPlate is an AI-powered web application designed to reduce food waste by connecting restaurants with nearby users in real time. Restaurants can list surplus food, and users can reserve it at discounted prices for self-pickup, ensuring that food is consumed instead of wasted.


## 🚀 Problem Statement

* Large amounts of food are wasted daily by restaurants
* Many people seek affordable food options
* Lack of a real-time system to connect surplus food with demand
* Food waste contributes to environmental issues



## 💡 Solution

EcoPlate bridges this gap by:

* Allowing restaurants to upload unsold food
* Enabling users to discover and reserve food at discounted prices
* Using AI to predict potential food waste
* Promoting a sustainable and efficient food distribution system



## 🧠 Key Features

* 🔐 Role-based authentication (User & Restaurant)
* 🍽 Restaurant dashboard to add and manage food listings
* 🛒 User dashboard to browse and reserve food
* ⏳ Food rescue concept (time-based pickup)
* 🤖 AI-based food waste prediction
* 🌍 Focus on sustainability and reducing environmental impact



## ⚙️ Tech Stack

**Frontend:**

* React.js
* Tailwind CSS

**Backend:**

* FastAPI (Python)

**Database:**

* Firebase / MongoDB

**AI Model:**

* Scikit-learn (Linear Regression)

**Authentication:**

* JWT / Firebase Auth



## 🏗️ System Workflow

1. Restaurant logs in and uploads leftover food
2. AI predicts potential food waste
3. Users browse available food items
4. Users reserve food at discounted prices
5. Food is picked up before expiry
